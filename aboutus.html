About SLFA

Learn more about our mission, vision, and commitment to football development in Sierra Leone

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a professional platform that connects talented football players with licensed agents, fostering career development and opportunities in Sierra Leone and beyond.

We strive to promote transparency, professionalism, and excellence in football representation while supporting the growth of local talent.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform for football representation in Sierra Leone, recognized for excellence in player development and agent services.

We envision a future where every talented player has access to professional representation and career opportunities.

Our Values

Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of honesty and ethical conduct in all our dealings.

Professionalism

We provide professional services that meet international standards and best practices.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from player representation to match coverage.

Development

We are committed to the continuous development of players and the football community.

Our History

SLFA was established to address the need for professional football representation in Sierra Leone. Over the years, we have successfully connected numerous talented players with licensed agents and professional opportunities.

Our platform has grown to become a trusted resource for players, agents, and clubs alike, fostering a vibrant football community in Sierra Leone.
